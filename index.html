3. Time Perception and Resonance: Our experience of time can be modulated through vibrational awareness and frequency tuning.
4. Collective Consciousness: Humanity’s unified resonance holds the potential to transform reality toward harmony and abundance.
5. Open Collaboration: Knowledge and discoveries must remain accessible, fostering cooperation and evolution.
